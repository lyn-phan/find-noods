Welcome to Find Noods!


This simple site is intended to make it easier for those who are visually impaired to have easier access to restaurant/vendor information. A lot of sites aren't compatible with screen readers, so this site will quickly look up vendor hours, contact info, if they partner with third party delivery apps, and a menu if its available. If you have any feedback on the app, please contact Lynda Phan via the contact form.

You can look up by address or vendor name.
